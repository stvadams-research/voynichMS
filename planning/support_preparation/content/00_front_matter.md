## Scope and Inferential Boundaries

This study does not assert the absence of meaning, nor does it claim the author's intent was fraudulent. It concludes only that non-semantic generative processes are structurally sufficient to explain the observed complexity of the text. The results establish a text-internal boundary for semantic inference: the observable structure does not support an inference of semantics under current diagnostics.
